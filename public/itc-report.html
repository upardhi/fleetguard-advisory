06 · Decision Brief · Board-Grade

ROI & Business Impact

A simple before/after view focused on control, traceability, and operational speed — without debated ₹ projections.

Before · No System current state
Pros
  • Lowest change-management overhead (works “as-is”).
  • No new SOP or training required.
Cons / risks
  • Zero real-time visibility across 37 warehouses — CSO relies on spreadsheets & calls.
  • Weak gate enforcement — manual checks and inconsistent records.
  • Paper-based POD — disputes take 3–7 days, revenue leakage in returns.
  • No structured gate timestamps · scheduling, billing & dispute cycles all rely on memory and paper.
  • No immutable audit trail for insurance / regulatory defence.
Summary: low effort today, but high operational risk and weak auditability.
After · With Fraudcheck.ai simplified impact view
Pros
  • Live pan-India command view — every gate, truck and alert in one console.
  • Stronger gate discipline — standardized flow reduces ad-hoc decisions.
  • Digital POD via QR + dealer PIN — disputes closed in minutes, not days.
  • Structured timestamps — entry/exit times become queryable operations data.
  • Immutable audit trail on every gate event — insurance & legal-grade.
Summary: higher upfront change, but materially stronger control, traceability, and operational clarity.
Recommendation
Roll out a controlled deployment: start with 1–2 DCs, lock SOP at the gate, and review weekly exceptions. Keep the value story operational (control, traceability, speed) — add ₹ ROI only after finance validates assumptions.
What improves first
Visibility + audit trail + standardized gate flow
What needs discipline
Adoption, data capture quality, exception handling
How to measure
Exit capture rate, dispute turnaround, cycle time, incident closure
